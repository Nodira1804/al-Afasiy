Creativity and achievements

Since 2003, he has been reciting verses from the Qur'an as an independent singer. Over the years, his audio recordings have spread around the world,capturing the hearts of Muslims because of his unique recitation of a beautiful voice.
On October 25, 2008,Sheikh Mishari al-Afasy was awarded the first prize of the First Arab Creative Union in a competition held by the Union of Arab Artists in Egypt.
In 2012, he was recognized as the best Qur'an reciter in a poll of Muslims.
